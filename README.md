# Shapeshifter Mod - Bedrock Edition

A Minecraft Bedrock mod that lets you shapeshift into blocks and entities by crouching and hitting them with your crosshair aimed at the target.

## Features

- **Shapeshift Mechanic**: Crouch and hit (attack) while looking at a block or entity to transform into it
- **Visual Transformation**: Your model changes to match the target
- **Crosshair Targeting**: You must have your crosshair aimed directly at the target to shapeshift
- **Revert**: Crouch and hit while in the air to return to your normal form

## Installation (Bedrock Edition)

1. Download the addon files
2. Open the folder and compress the `behavior_packs` and `resource_packs` folders
3. Rename to `.mcaddon` format
4. Open the file with Minecraft to install
5. Enable the addon in world settings

## How to Use

1. Look directly at a block or entity with your crosshair centered on it
2. Crouch (Sneak - default Shift on keyboard, Right Trigger on controller)
3. Attack (Left-click on keyboard, Right Trigger on controller)
4. Crouch and attack in the air to revert to human form

## Compatibility

- Minecraft Bedrock Edition 1.20+
